Google Sheets Python UUID Generator

Output — Immediate
Instant Confirmation Message
Within 60 seconds of booking, patient receives a WhatsApp confirmation with: doctor name, date, time, clinic address, and booking reference number.
Twilio WhatsApp API Make Scenario
⏰
Action
Schedule Reminder Sequence
The system schedules 3 automated reminder messages based on appointment time: 24-hour reminder, 2-hour reminder, and post-visit follow-up.
Python APScheduler Make Schedule Module
SCHEDULED REMINDER SEQUENCE:
24 hrs before → “Reminder: Your appointment with [Doctor] is tomorrow at [Time]. Reply CONFIRM or CANCEL.”
2 hrs before → “Your appointment is in 2 hours! Please arrive 10 mins early. Clinic: [Address]”
2 hrs after → “How was your visit today? Please rate your experience (1-5) — your feedback helps us improve!”
🕐
Decision
Patient Confirms or Cancels?
When patient replies to the 24-hr reminder, the system detects CONFIRM or CANCEL and routes accordingly.
Make Text Parser Keyword Detection
CONFIRMATION BRANCHES:
CONFIRM → Send “Great! See you tomorrow. Bring your previous prescriptions if any.” + mark confirmed in Sheet
CANCEL → Send “Appointment cancelled. Would you like to reschedule?” + free up slot in Sheet + notify receptionist
No Reply → Send one follow-up after 4 hours, then mark as “Unconfirmed” in Sheet
⭐
AI Processing
Analyze Feedback & Generate Response
Post-visit feedback is analyzed by AI. Happy patients (4-5 stars) are prompted to leave a Google Review. Others receive a personalized apology & resolution message.
ChatGPT API Sentiment Analysis Python
🌟
